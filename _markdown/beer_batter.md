# Beer Batter

Tags: American, Vegetarian, Entree



## Ingredients

240 grams flour

1/3 cup cornstarch

6 grams salt

3 grams paprika

1 tbsp baking powder

12 oz. beer



## Instructions

Whisk dry ingredients. Add beer and incorporate. Mixture should feel like pancake batter.

Cook 2-3 minutes per side in 375 F oil.

# Pancakes

Tags: American, Breakfast, Vegetarian



## Ingredients

120 grams flour

2  tbsp sugar

1  tsp baking powder

1/2  tsp baking soda

1/2  tsp salt

1  egg

2  tbsp butter

1  cup buttermilk



## Instructions

Combine dry ingredients.

Melt butter, mix with egg and buttermilk; whisk well.

Cook on griddle.

# Banana Bread

Tags: American, Vegetarian, Fruit



## Ingredients

1/4 cup butter

1/8 cup sugar

1 large egg

1 ripe banana

1/8 tsp of salt

1/2 tsp baking soda

60 grams flour



## Instructions

Preheat oven to 300 F.

Cream butter and sugar.

Combine all dry ingredients and mix well.

Combine all remaining ingredients and mix well.

Bake for 35 minutes.
